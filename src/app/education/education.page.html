Tips on Blood Donation

Before Donation

1. Get a good night's sleep before donating.

2. Eat a healthy meal and drink plenty of fluids.

3. Bring identification and any required forms.

During Donation

1. Relax and stay calm during the donation process.

2. Inform staff if you experience any discomfort.

3. Stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activities afterward.

After Donation

1. Rest for a few minutes after donating.

2. Eat a snack and drink fluids to replenish.

3. Avoid heavy lifting or vigorous exercise for the rest of the day.
