About Us

Our Mission

Blood Connect is dedicated to bridging the gap between blood donors and those in need. Our mission is to create a seamless and convenient platform that connects people willing to donate blood with hospitals, blood banks, and individuals urgently requiring blood transfusions.

Our Story

Blood Connect was founded in 2022 by a team of passionate individuals who recognized the challenges faced by both blood donors and recipients. We saw a need for a centralized platform that could streamline the blood donation process and make it easier for people to find and access the blood they need.
